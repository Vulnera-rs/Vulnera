# Getting Support

## Community Support (Free)

- **GitHub Discussions:** [Vulnera-rs/Vulnera/discussions](https://github.com/Vulnera-rs/Vulnera/discussions) — Ask questions, share ideas, get help from the community.
- **GitHub Issues:** [Vulnera-rs/Vulnera/issues](https://github.com/Vulnera-rs/Vulnera/issues) — Report bugs and request features.

## Enterprise Support (Paid)

Enterprise customers with an active subscription receive:

- **Priority issue triage** — Dedicated response within 1 business day
- **Private Slack/Teams channel** — Direct access to the Vulnera engineering team
- **Custom module development** — Tailored analysis modules for your stack
- **On-call support** — Critical vulnerability response SLA
- **Deployment assistance** — Help with on-premise and hybrid cloud setups

Contact **<enterprise@vulnera.studio>** for pricing and availability.

## Sponsorship

Vulnera is developed by Vulnera Industries, a for-profit company.

If you'd like to financially support the project, you can do so via [GitHub Sponsors](https://github.com/sponsors/Vulnera-rs). Sponsorships go directly to Vulnera Industries and are used as general company revenue. There are no perks or entitlements associated with sponsorship.
